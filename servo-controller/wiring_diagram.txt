/*
 * WIRING DIAGRAM - ESP32-C6 Servo Pan-Tilt Bracket Controller
 * 
 * ESP32-C6 DevKit Connections:
 * =============================
 * 
 *    ESP32-C6         Pan Servo (SG90)         Tilt Servo (SG90)
 *    --------         ----------------         ------------------
 *    GPIO 18  ------>  Signal (Orange)
 *    GPIO 19  ------------------------->      Signal (Orange)  
 *    Ext 5V   ------>  VCC (Red)       ------>  VCC (Red)
 *    GND      ------>  GND (Brown)     ------>  GND (Brown)
 * 
 * ESP32-C6 Pinout Reference:
 * ==========================
 *                   ESP32-C6 DevKitC-1
 *                  ┌─────────────────────┐
 *           3V3 ──┤1                  21├── GPIO21
 *           RST ──┤2                  20├── GPIO20  
 *         GPIO4 ──┤3                  19├── GPIO19 (Tilt Servo)
 *         GPIO5 ──┤4                  18├── GPIO18 (Pan Servo)
 *         GPIO6 ──┤5                  17├── GPIO10
 *         GPIO7 ──┤6                  16├── GPIO9
 *       GPIO15 ──┤7    [USB-C]        15├── GPIO8 (Built-in LED)
 *       GPIO16 ──┤8                  14├── GPIO3
 *       GPIO17 ──┤9                  13├── GPIO2
 *         GPIO22 ──┤10                12├── GPIO1
 *         GPIO23 ──┤11   ESP32-C6     11├── GPIO0 (BOOT)
 *           GND ──┤12  (RISC-V)      10├── GND
 *                  └─────────────────────┘
 * 
 * 
 * Physical Setup:
 * ===============
 * 
 * 1. Pan Servo (Base/Bottom):
 *    - Mounted horizontally on base
 *    - Controls left-right movement (0° = left, 90° = center, 180° = right)
 *    - Servo horn attached to vertical bracket
 * 
 * 2. Tilt Servo (Top):
 *    - Mounted on pan servo's moving bracket
 *    - Controls up-down movement (0° = up, 90° = center, 180° = down)
 *    - Camera/payload attached to tilt servo horn
 * 
 * 
 * Power Considerations:
 * =====================
 * 
 * Option 1 - Arduino Power (Light loads only):
 * └── Use Arduino 5V pin for both servos
 * └── Maximum ~500mA total current
 * └── Suitable for SG90 micro servos without heavy loads
 * 
 * Option 2 - External Power (Recommended):
 * ┌── External 5V Power Supply (2A+)
 * ├── Connect VCC of both servos to external 5V
 * ├── Connect Arduino GND to external power GND
 * └── Keep Arduino powered separately via USB or Vin
 * 
 * 
 * Bracket Assembly Order:
 * =======================
 * 
 * 1. Base Platform
 * 2. Pan Servo (horizontal, controls rotation)
 * 3. Vertical Bracket (attached to pan servo horn)
 * 4. Tilt Servo (mounted on vertical bracket)
 * 5. Camera/Payload Mount (attached to tilt servo horn)
 * 
 * 
 * Servo Angle Reference:
 * ======================
 * 
 * Pan Servo (Horizontal Movement):
 *   0° = Full Left
 *  90° = Center
 * 180° = Full Right
 * 
 * Tilt Servo (Vertical Movement):
 *   0° = Full Up
 *  90° = Center/Level
 * 180° = Full Down
 * 
 * 
 * Demo Movement Patterns:
 * =======================
 * 
 * Phase 1 - Pan Sweep:
 * Pan: 0° → 180° → 0° (sweeping)
 * Tilt: 90° (fixed center)
 * 
 * Phase 2 - Combined Movement:
 * Pan: 0° ↔ 180° (continuous)
 * Tilt: 0° ↔ 180° (oscillating)
 * 
 * Phase 3 - Circular Motion:
 * Creates smooth circular patterns using sine/cosine
 * Center point: Pan=90°, Tilt=90°
 * Radius: ±30° from center
 * 
 * Phase 4 - Random Positioning:
 * Both servos move to random positions
 * Changes every 2 seconds
 * Cycles through 5 random positions before restarting
 * 
 */
